Super Administrator [ Help Index ]

The SuperAdministrator is the highest position among all the Administrators and allows you to control the entire system. The SuperAdministrator has the authority to access any part of the system. The SuperAdministrator is responsible for creating other Administrators, controlling the Access Rights and changing the Designation of the Administrators. It is especially helpful in assigning WebAdmin tasks to selected users.

You can also assign Sub-Administrators quotas for the accounts they create or the disk-space they use. This is useful if you want to limit the capabilities of a SubAdmin.
